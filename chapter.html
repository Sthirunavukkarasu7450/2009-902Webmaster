What is TSA?
TSA (Technology Student Association) is an organization that helps to nurture future leaders, engineers, problem solvers, entrepreneurs and more. With almost a quarter million members, TSA is growing in to be one of the biggest student led oragnizations. With many events to choose from, students have a large variety of selection from events such as webmaster, to extemporaneous speech, to children's stories. It has been running for more than 40 years with many famous members who have graduated out of it such as Mark Zuckerburg of Meta (New York TSA), Sal Khan of Khan Academy(Lousiana TSA), Chad Hurley(Pennsylvania TSA), and many more.
