Who are we?

Founded in early 2020, we hoped to bring back the world of music from isolation. Over the course of the pandemic, we have run these virtual sessions to help young artists to grow as musicians as many could not interact with fellow band members or teachers in order to improve their playing. We wanted this to be something that they could still be engaged in music as many of the world shut down. As the pandemic went on, more and more people tended to drop playing an instrument or learing about music since they had no place to go and exhibit it. Here, we hope to let people not only learn, but to also play would their fellow instrument compatriats and have that social interaction many of them had been missing over these trying times.

Our Mission:

Our motto, Striving for Musical Excellence Through Collaboration and Compassion, represents our mission as a whole organization. We hope to bring music to people of all ages and regions of the world regardless of money, race, gender, etc. Music should bring us all together and this is what we strive for.

The Drive Behind the Society:

Besides having a goal, no organization can keep themselves going without reason and purpose. Many of our board members were once excellent musicians themselves. They saw music as a part of that has made them better people today. Music does that for many people. It helps them get through in their lives. It can be used to celebrate something as well as mourn for a loss. Within the past two years, the COVID-19 Pandemic has truly shocked the world altogether. Many people turned to music as a means to keep going. We hoped to bring people together through music once more through the use of our instrument workshops and music programs where people can not only improve themselves as musicians, but also grow to be better human beings.
